About Me

Hi. Thank you for taking the time to stop by and view my portfolio. I'd like to share a little more about myself. I'm in the process of learning Web Development through KU's Coding Bootcamp. Before starting down this path I took some continuing education courses at Johnson County Community College.

I live in Overland Park, KS with my husband and two pups. We enjoy movies and are boardgame geeks.
