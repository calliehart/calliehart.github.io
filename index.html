Films Plus Friends

Films + Friends is a service aimed at helping users plan what movies to watch with their friends. The user selects a genre and rating and we return 5 movies from the OMDB API. These results are editable allowing the user to remove any movies and then add in one of their choice through a search. The app then sends out invitations for everyone invited to vote for the movie of their choice. Once all votes are in everyone receives an email with the winning title.
